Site Name

• Reason for Selection: The name highlights freshness and quality, aligning with the business’s commitment to providing premium chicken products. It is memorable, simple, and conveys trust.

Site Purpose
The purpose of the The Poultry Farmer website is to serve as a central hub for customers seeking high-quality poultry products and recipes.
The site will:
• Provide information about the company, including its values and farming practices.
• Offer an online store for convenient product purchases.
• Share chicken recipes, cooking tips, and product preparation guides.
• Facilitate customer inquiries and engagement via a contact form.
